# P1-F9-T01: Unanet CRM Connector Scope and Business Role

## Scope

- Unanet CRM is a Wave 2 opportunity and CRM context source.
- Its role is to enrich HB Intel pursuit, opportunity, and customer/project context through published read models instead of direct operational writes.

## Target Consumers

- Business-development and project-initiation publications
- Project identity and cross-source opportunity context

## Out of Scope

- No direct CRM UI embedding or direct feature-package calls in this pass.
