# 08 — Prompt: Validation and Closure

## Mission

Validate the targeted 100% zoom compaction / overflow work and refuse closure until the public HB Kudos surface is visually stronger and measurably safer in the host.

## Closure standard

The work is only complete if all of the following are true:

1. The current premium concept is preserved.
2. The masthead / featured zone is visibly shorter and better balanced at 100% zoom.
3. The start of recent recognition appears earlier in the first viewport.
4. Archive/lower-zone continuation remains authored and proportionate after compaction.
5. Hosted top and bottom safe zones are handled cleanly.
6. The implementation remains aligned with ui-kit guidance.
7. The Playwright webparts harness has been updated and passing for the changed conditions.

## Required validation checklist

### Visual
- Compare before/after at desktop 100%.
- Compare before/after at desktop 90%.
- Review reduced-width hosted desktop.
- Review iPhone 12 Pro hosted view.
- Confirm the webpart still feels premium, not merely smaller.

### Structural
- Confirm the compaction work was applied in the correct seams.
- Confirm neighboring affected elements were not left on mismatched old sizing.
- Confirm no accidental override sprawl was introduced.

### Interaction
- Confirm changed visible controls remain clearly operable.
- Confirm focus-visible behavior.
- Confirm no dead CTAs.

### Hosted
- Confirm top chrome coexistence.
- Confirm bottom-right assistant-overlay safe zone.
- Confirm archive/footer content is not visually compromised by the overlay area.

## Required handoff note

Provide a concise closure note covering:
- what was compacted
- what downstream areas were rebalanced
- what hosted protections were added
- what ui-kit seams were updated
- what harness specs were added or changed
- what proof artifacts were captured

## Refusal rule

Do not claim success if the result is:
- just smaller but not better,
- only partially adjusted across the affected area,
- not validated at 100% zoom,
- or not aligned with ui-kit guidance.
