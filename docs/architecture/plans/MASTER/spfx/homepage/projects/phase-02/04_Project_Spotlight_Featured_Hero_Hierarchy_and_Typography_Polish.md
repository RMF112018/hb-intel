# Prompt 04 — Project Spotlight Featured Hero Hierarchy and Typography Polish

## Objective

Upgrade the featured Project Spotlight surface from a clean card-like layout into a true homepage flagship story surface aligned with the Signature Hero quality bar.

---

## Required design direction

The Project Spotlight architecture calls for:
- one dominant featured project
- strong image-led hierarchy
- 70 / 30 desktop balance
- premium portfolio behavior
- compact supporting metadata
- no dashboard drift

Implement that direction directly.

---

## Required work

### A. Desktop hierarchy
Move the featured/rail relationship toward a true **70 / 30** editorial balance unless repo/runtime constraints prove a slightly adjusted ratio is necessary.

### B. Featured image dominance
Increase the authority of the featured visual region.
Possible approved approaches:
- stronger image zone proportion
- lower content shelf overlay
- floating content pane over image
- other doctrine-compliant premium editorial treatment

Choose the best repo-compatible option.

### C. Typography hierarchy
Upgrade:
- section heading presence
- featured project title scale and authority
- highlight headline clarity
- summary readability and restraint
- metadata quietness

### D. Surface treatment
Refine:
- depth
- border/radius logic
- spacing rhythm
- section separation
- premium but restrained motion

### E. CTA hierarchy
Keep the featured CTA clear and premium without clutter.

---

## Guardrails

Do not:
- turn the surface into a dashboard card
- add more metadata to compensate for weak hierarchy
- over-animate the module
- create hero mimicry that feels repetitive instead of compatible

---

## Deliverables

### 1. Files changed
### 2. Before/after design summary
Explain exactly how the featured hierarchy was strengthened.
### 3. Validation
At minimum:
- typecheck/build
- visual proof the featured story is clearly dominant
- proof that hierarchy remains strong at desktop and tablet breakpoints

---

## Hard constraints

- Stay tightly scoped to the featured surface and its immediate composition.
- Do not yet rework the rail in depth except where needed to maintain balance.
