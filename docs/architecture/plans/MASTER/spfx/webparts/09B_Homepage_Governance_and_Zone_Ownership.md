# 09B — Homepage Governance and Zone Ownership

## Purpose

Define Prompt-09 governance boundaries so homepage content remains curated, fresh, and protected from sprawl.

## Zone Purpose and Ownership

- Top Band (`personalizedWelcomeHeader`, `hbHeroBanner`)
  - Owner: Corporate/Executive Communications
  - Purpose: signature greeting and primary message
  - Freshness: daily-to-weekly based on active notices
- Utility Zone (`priorityActionsRail`, `toolLauncherWorkHub`)
  - Owner: Operations Program Managers
  - Purpose: high-intent task execution and system launch
  - Freshness: daily-to-weekly based on operational workload
- Awareness Zone (`companyPulse`, `leadershipMessage`, `peopleCulture`)
  - Owner: Communications + People Operations
  - Purpose: curated updates, leadership narrative, and culture highlights
  - Freshness: weekly rotation with bounded hierarchy
- Operational Awareness Zone (`projectPortfolioSpotlight`, `safetyFieldExcellence`)
  - Owner: Project Controls + Safety/Field Excellence
  - Purpose: business and field signals with status/freshness context
  - Freshness: weekly review with stale-item controls
- Discovery Zone (`smartSearchWayfinding`)
  - Owner: Operations Enablement
  - Purpose: guided discovery for tools/forms/policies/teams/destinations
  - Freshness: weekly review of promoted and quick-path relevance

## Anti-Sprawl Rules

- No zone should become a routed mini-app or dashboard clone.
- Keep featured-vs-secondary hierarchy explicit where editorial or operational weighting is required.
- Keep promoted destinations bounded and high-intent; avoid long unstructured link lists.
- Preserve homepage-safe import guardrails and lightweight render behavior.
