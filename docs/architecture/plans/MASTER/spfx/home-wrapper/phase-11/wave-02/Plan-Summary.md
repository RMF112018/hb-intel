Complete the application-level fit work required to keep the locked three-row homepage shell stable across non-handheld widths.

## Primary risk
The shell cannot paper over application-level fit failures. If a child surface cannot survive the subordinate slot, the page will still violate the requested target state.

## Boundaries
- Do not redesign applications for unrelated reasons.

- Do not re-read files already in active context unless needed to confirm drift, dependencies, or uncertainty after changes.
