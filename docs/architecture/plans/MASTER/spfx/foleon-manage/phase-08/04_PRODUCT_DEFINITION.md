# 04 — Product Definition

## Product Name

**Foleon Feed Manager**

Keep the SharePoint page title as “Foleon Manager” if required, but the in-app title should express purpose:

> Foleon Feed Manager

Subtitle:

> Place Foleon-produced content into HB Central feeds, schedule display windows, and validate what employees will see.

## Product Role

The Manager is the placement, scheduling, readiness, and preview console for HB Central feed content created in Foleon.

## What This App Is

- A feed operations desk.
- A content placement manager.
- A publishing readiness console.
- A source-sync and validation surface.
- A controlled bridge between Foleon and HB Central.

## What This App Is Not

- Not a Foleon authoring replacement.
- Not a general dashboard.
- Not a backend diagnostics console.
- Not a lane-board experiment.
- Not a SharePoint list editor.
- Not a collection of cards.

## Primary Personas

### Marketing Content Manager

Responsibilities:
- verify new Foleon content is available,
- assign content to the correct HB Central feed,
- set display windows,
- validate readiness,
- preview employee-facing output,
- activate or stage content.

Needs:
- simple queue,
- clear statuses,
- obvious next action,
- preview confidence,
- minimal technical noise.

### HB Intel Admin

Responsibilities:
- configure list bindings,
- validate backend readiness,
- resolve API/OAuth issues,
- review diagnostics,
- confirm package/runtime proof.

Needs:
- separate admin workspace,
- redacted proof,
- readiness checks,
- no exposure to regular content managers unless required.

## Product Questions the UI Must Answer

- What came from Foleon?
- What needs attention?
- What is ready to place?
- What is live now?
- What is scheduled next?
- What is blocked and who fixes it?
- What will employees see?
- What changed since last sync?
- Is the system ready enough to publish?
