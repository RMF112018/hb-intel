## Completion Gate

A BuyoutPackage is not complete until vendor, award, LOI/subcontract/PO, Procore commitment, compliance, reconciliation, procurement-risk, and source-lineage gates are satisfied or formally waived.

## Waiver Policy

Any waiver requires reason, approver, timestamp, and evidence. PX approval is required for SDI waiver, bond waiver, over-budget completion, or completion with unresolved procurement risk.
