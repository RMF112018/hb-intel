# Future-Wave Handoff Notes

## Wave 10 — Permit Log

Wave 9 may show permit/AHJ readiness posture and link/reference permit obligations, but Wave 10 owns detailed permit workflow, permit records, AHJ tracking, and permit-specific lifecycle logic.

## Wave 11 — Responsibility Matrix / RACI

Wave 9 may assign owners/reviewers and show accountable-party gaps, but Wave 11 owns the full Responsibility Matrix / RACI module and seed responsibility library.

## Wave 12 — Constraints Log

Wave 9 may surface readiness blockers and external dependencies, but Wave 12 owns the detailed constraints log and constraint resolution workflow.

## Wave 13 — Buyout Log

Wave 9 may reference buyout readiness exposure, especially long-lead/startup controls, but Wave 13 owns buyout tracking workflow.

## Wave 14 — Approvals / Checkpoints

Wave 9 may create read-model checkpoint posture and disabled/inert checkpoint affordances, but Wave 14 owns actual approval/checkpoint execution behavior.

## Later Integrations

Live Graph, SharePoint, Procore, Sage, Outlook, Adobe Sign, Document Crunch, Safety, notification, and workflow integrations are explicitly deferred until later authorization.
