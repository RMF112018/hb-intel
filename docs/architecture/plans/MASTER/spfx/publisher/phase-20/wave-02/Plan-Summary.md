# Plan Summary — Wave 02

## Objective
Advance the Publisher from a strong governed authoring workspace into a more mature flagship editorial product without introducing gratuitous complexity.

## Work units
1. Reassess story-authoring capability against real page/template needs.
2. Strengthen preview fidelity and preview-to-fix confidence.
3. Deepen project-context-driven assistance where authoritative context justifies it.
4. Clean residual naming/seam drift and harden maintainability/accessibility proof.

## Closure standard
Wave 02 is only closed when the product feels not just correct and governed, but materially more natural and premium in daily editorial use.
