# Plan Summary

## Objective
Turn the Spotlight from an empty-image-led module into a credible featured-project surface in the first row of the homepage.

## Closure target
After this wave:

- the featured image path is proven and either works or degrades intentionally
- missing-media states no longer consume the major slot as a giant billboard
- the featured title and at least one meaningful project signal land materially higher in the viewport on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- the runtime looks better in hosted SharePoint, not just in stories
