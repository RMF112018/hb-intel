## 5. Phase 4 Gate

PCC should not enter Phase 4 UI/UX validation unless:

- Final score is 95/100 or higher.
- No hard-stop failures remain.
- No pillar scores below 80% of available points.
- Evidence is complete and reproducible.
- Final score is expert-reviewed and not auto-generated by Playwright.

## 8. Scoring Authority Rule

Playwright generates evidence, traceability, worksheets, findings, and audit packages.

Playwright must not be treated as the final scoring authority. Final scores and hard-stop dispositions remain expert-reviewed.
